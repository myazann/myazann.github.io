I'm a PhD candidate, enrolled at Hogeschool van Amsterdam and Leiden University. My research focuses on tailoring chatbots responses to users' personal and emotional needs, under different tasks and domains. My areas of interest are:

  1. Understanding how emotions, personality, and the context shape individual differences in user perception of chatbots
  2. Exploring the effect of personalization and anthropomorphism on user perception of chatbots
  3. Understanding how and why people interact with chatbots
  4. Designing personalized and adaptive chatbots

Continuing my PhD, I aim to bridge AI and user psychology to design better user experiences. With a BSc in Computer Engineering and an MSc in Computer Vision, I now focus on NLP, working with LLMs and RAG.
