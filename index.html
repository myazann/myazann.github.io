I'm a PhD candidate, enrolled at Hogeschool van Amsterdam and Leiden University. My research is interdimensional, bridging consumer psychology with chatbots.

I focus on:

  1. Personalization of LLMs
  2. Aligning chatbot behavior to the consumer psychology in various domains and settings
  3. Knowledge transfer of chatbots, specifically for low-resource domains

I have experience in different areas of AI, having completed my Master's in Computer Vision. I also worked as a data scientist for a bank, designing credit scoring models. Nowadays, I work mostly with increasing the personalization capabilities of LLMs so that chatbots can match the conversation style of their users.
